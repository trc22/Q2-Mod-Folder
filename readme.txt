CHANGES:

Heat meter:
-If heat gets to 25, the player is frozen. If heat gets to 175, player is burning
-Tic damage for freeze/burn is
-Burning players radiate heat and explode on death 
-Particle effect for burning players
-Frozen players move extremely slow
-Water now stops burning and slowly lowers temp
-Freezing in the water does extra damage


Ice physics:

-Player accelerates/decelerates slowly


Weapons:

-Blaster lowers enemy heat
-Hyperblaster lowers target heat and player heat
-Machine Gun raises target heat
-Chain gun raises target heat and player heat
-Shotgun raises target heat and lowers player heat
-Super shotgun lowers target heat and raises player heat
-Grenades / Grenade launcher raise heat in aoe
-Rocket launcher lowers heat in aoe
-BFG freezes everything and resets player heat


Area specific damage:

-Headshots do double damage
-Body shots do normal damage
-Leg shots do 25% less damage


UI change:

-Thermometer instead of hp 

Particle effect change:
-Blaser and rocket launcher have blue effects
-Burning players spawn particle effects per tic
